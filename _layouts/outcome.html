Protect tenants and increase the supply of homes that low- and middle-income households can afford

  • Update our zoning laws to mandate multi-family, inclusionary zoning, and cluster development. Prohibity exclusionary zoning.
  • Create a universal application for housing.
  • Incentivize affordable housing near transit.
  • Create protections for tenants: right to counsel, right of first refusal, housing tax credits to lessor that rent at below-market rates.
